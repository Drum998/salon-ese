About Billing Elements

Billing Elements are used to break down service costs for commission calculations. Each element represents a percentage of the total service cost that can be allocated to different aspects of the service (e.g., color, styling, treatments).

Total Percentage: The sum of all active billing elements should equal 100% for accurate commission calculations.

Commission Calculation
How it works:
  1. Each service is broken down into billing elements
  2. Stylist commission is calculated based on their commission rate
  3. Commission applies to the stylist's portion of each element
  4. Total commission = Sum of (element amount × commission rate)
Example:
  • Service: £100
  • Color (25%): £25
  • Styling (20%): £20
  • Stylist Commission Rate: 70%
  • Total Commission: £31.50
